This week's release focuses on expanding Hazel's integration capabilities with full Discord sync support, including two-way attachment syncing. We've also added GIF support and custom emojis to make conversations more expressive. Desktop notifications keep you informed, and the new arrow-up shortcut for editing messages brings a familiar workflow from other chat apps. Under the hood, we've made significant performance improvements to keep the app feeling fast and responsive.
